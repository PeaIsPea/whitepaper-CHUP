CHUP - Web3 Survival Game

Developed by LamTuePea | Built on BNB Chain

Overview Tokenomics NFT & Gear Web3 Integration Community

1. Game Overview

CHUP is a hardcore survival Web3 game where players start as powerless civilians in one of four kingdoms. With a "one-life" mechanic and rich profession system, every decision can mean survival or defeat. The game features 40 maps, diverse missions, national wars, and PvE/PvP battlefields.

2. Dual-Token Economy

$PHC - Main token for trading, rewards, and NFT marketplace.

$PCOIN - Sub-token used for upgrading skills, gear, and items.

  • Burn mechanisms to reduce inflation (5% fee on $PHC transactions).
  • Leveling system rewards both tokens up to level 20.

3. NFT & Legendary Weapons

All equipment including weapons, gear, houses, and tools are NFTs. Gear can be upgraded from level 0 to 50. Legendary weapons (3 unique items) are dropped randomly and visible on-chain. If the owner dies and the weapon is not claimed in 15 minutes, the item is reclaimed by the server for future events.

4. Web3 Integration

Guest login enabled. Players can later link Web3 wallets for NFT & token management. All drops, trades, and upgrades are verifiable on the BNB Chain.

DAO governance will allow players to vote on events, map expansions, and rule changes.

Zero-Knowledge Proof for Legendary Weapon Drops

To ensure fairness and transparency, CHUP utilizes zero-knowledge proofs (ZKP) to validate that legendary weapon drops are conducted randomly and without manipulation.

  • Drop events are seeded with unpredictable data (e.g. block hash, server time).
  • A zk-SNARK proof is generated to confirm the drop was executed fairly without exposing the internal logic.
  • The proof is published and verifiable on-chain or accessible via game logs.
  • This prevents tampering and reassures the community that even developers cannot rig drops.

5. Community & World Events

  • Each kingdom represents a national identity (great for community building).
  • Guild system, profession rankings, and cooperative events like wars and raids.
  • In global events, players from each kingdom cooperate under one flag despite internal guild competition.
